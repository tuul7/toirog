Our Heritage

Where the Steppe
Meets Artistry

Founded in 2008 by master craftswoman Oyuntsetseg Battulga, Nomadic Felt Studio preserves the ancient Mongolian art of felt-making — esgii urlag — using only 100% natural wool sourced from nomadic herders across the Mongolian steppe.

Every piece is hand-felted using wet-felting and needle-felting techniques passed down through generations. No machines. No shortcuts. Just wool, water, and skilled hands that know the ancient rhythms of this craft.

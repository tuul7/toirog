Our Story

More than a
thrift store.

Founded in 2020, TOIROG was born from a mission to shift how Mongolians think about second-hand clothing. We're not just selling old clothes — we're building a community around sustainable, mindful consumption and keeping beautiful pieces in circulation.

"Хуучин биш, хайрлаж эдэлсэн" — Not old, pre-loved.
Sustainable
Every purchase extends a garment's life and reduces textile waste.
Affordable
Quality fashion that doesn't break the bank — for everyone.
Unique
Curated vintage pieces with character you won't find elsewhere.
Community
Donate, shop, connect — thrifting as a shared experience.
How It Works

Simple as three steps.

Whether you're shopping or donating, TOIROG makes it effortless to be part of the sustainable fashion cycle.

Donate Your Clothes

Bring your pre-loved garments to our store. All conditions welcome — we handle the rest with care.

We Curate & Style

Our team carefully selects, cleans, and styles each piece so it finds its next perfect owner.

Shop & Discover

Browse our ever-changing floor and find one-of-a-kind pieces that tell a story.

The Cycle Continues

When you're done loving it, bring it back. Fashion should move, not pile up.

Eco Impact

Fashion with a conscience.

Every item at TOIROG is a step toward a more sustainable Mongolia. By choosing pre-loved over new, you help reduce water waste, carbon emissions, and the growing textile crisis.
